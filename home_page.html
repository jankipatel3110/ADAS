As we know a large number of deaths are caused by accidents.The global crisis of road safety can be seen by observing the significant number of deaths and injuries that are caused by road accidents. In many situations the family members or emergency services are not informed in time.Using this system our main objective is to control the accident by sending a message to the registered mobile using wireless communication techniques. Our project provides information about accident detection and prevention of human life safety.



Right now vehicle is the main mode of any type of transportation. There is a need of proper ride as well as security is the main issue today's life's. There is a no any system which alert when accident was happened. There is a need to design system that will help to victim who suffering for accident. This system design help to send actual accident location and also send emergency message to people who specified in emergency contact list of victim. It is possible an automatic vehicle accident detection by using GPS and GMS module design. We are concerned with the method to use GPS technology. The system includes a sensor, sound meter, GPS, GPRS, Microcontroller unit and GSM. If an accident accrues in rural area this application is more useful for victims. This system provides fast availability of safety.


This system will minimize the time gap and ensure medical assistance immediately. Various sensors in the smartphone will help in identifying the site of the accident like GPS technology and the mobile will send the message to ambulance and police station in case of accident like GSM. The medical team will get the location with the support of inbuilt sensors in smartphone through microcontroller unit. This will enable the response team to arrive at the accident side and provide medical support to victim in time. Then it has a great importance in day to day life of the people in the country like India. This proposed work will provide victim information about the accident even in unpopulated area. So, the pre-configured contacts could be able to serve to the victims with better efficiency and they could plan to have important first aid kits which have to be brought along with them to the accident spot. Thus this work ensures the reeducation of death ratio and fatalities in the country like India and also which will have a greater importance in day to day life.





The system includes a sensor, sound meter, GPS (Global Positioning System), GSM (Global System for Mobile Communications), GPRS (General Packet Radio Service), Microcontroller unit. The sensor will detect the accident & sound meter will trigger an alarm. GPS will show the location. GSM is used to carry messages to phone networks. GPRS is mobile internet and the microcontroller unit is the heart of system its receive data from GPS and process all data and then system will quickly assigns and sends notification to the nearby ambulance, nearby hospital and also the police informing in case of an accident detected.
